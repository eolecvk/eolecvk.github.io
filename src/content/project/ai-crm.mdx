## Building an AI Assistant for a Legal Team

TL;DR -- ***I used AI to automate the Client Relationship Management (CRM) for a legal team.  
The solution leverages LLMs and integrates with MS Outlook and a self-hosted CRM to automate administration and reporting of client opportunities.***

The design prioritizes **simplicity** (non-technical users needed to operate and manage the system), **data ownership** (confidential legal data must remain under their control), and to **avoid vendor lock-in** (open system, independent of any specific SaaS vendor).

[The project code available on GitHub](https://github.com/eolecvk/trustlink-workflow) includes scripts for integrating Microsoft Graph API, and the logic for triggering CRM updates based on incoming emails.
